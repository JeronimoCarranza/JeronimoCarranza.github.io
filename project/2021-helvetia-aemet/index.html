Implementation of improvements in the prediction model of atmospheric phenomena of HELVETIA.

 

Position: Head of project.
Recipients: HELVETIA COMPAÑÍA SUIZA, S.A. DE SEGUROS Y REASEGUROS. 2021.

The work carried out corresponds to the optimization of the procedures for downloading and using weather forecast data and AEMET alerts at a municipal scale, and has consisted in summary of:

  • The analysis and implementation of a program for the realization of web scraping of the data of interest to the AEMET related to weather forecasting and alerts at the municipal level, as well as its transformation, adaptation and persistence in a database for its subsequent exploitation and internal diffusion.
  • The tools used in the development of the works have been: R programming language, SQLite database.
