Computer technical assistance in the deployment, management and maintenance of servers, OGC map services and WebGIS viewer for the public exhibition of the review of floodplains of the Júcar River Basin District.

 

Posición: Head of project.
Destinatarios: TYPSA for Júcar River Basin Authority. 2020.

The work carried out has consisted in summary of:

  • Domain registration revision-snczi-jucar.es.
  • Linux VPS Server Configuration and Deployment of Base Applications.
  • GIS Server Configuration: Geoserver.
  • Publication of OGC Services: WFS / WCS / WMS.
  • Adaptation, Deployment, Configuration and Personalization of Basic WebGIS Client.
  • Monthly maintenance for thirteen months.
