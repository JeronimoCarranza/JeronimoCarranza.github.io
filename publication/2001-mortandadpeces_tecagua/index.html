Ecological characterization of the effects of a fish mortality event in the lower stretch of the Barbate River (Cádiz).

J. Prenda, M. Práxedes, J. Carranza, J. Ruiz

Abstract

This article describe the research about the event of a fish mortality occurred in may 1998, in the lower stretch of the Barbate River, as a consequence of the toxic spill of plaguicides used in the nearby ricefields.
