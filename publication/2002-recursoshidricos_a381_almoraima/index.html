Characterization of compensatory measures of the A-381 highway, Jerez-Los Barrios. Actions related to water resources.

M.A. Ruiz, E. Gordo, J. Carranza, J. Ruiz

Abstract

The purpose of this article is the description of the environmental compensatory measures of the effect caused by the Jerez-Los Barrios highway in the Los Alcornocales Natural Park related with to hydric resources. The measures include: paths for otters, artifial islands, refuges, little lakes, drinking troughs, and informative posters.
